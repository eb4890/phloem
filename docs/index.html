Intro

Phloem is a project to design a decentralised protocol for use in energy management. The times it would be useful would be:
  • Load management: Using electricity at the right times. For cost or availability reasons.
  • Energy resource discovery: Finding the available electricity generators and electricity storage mechanisms
  • Predicting shortfalls: Given the predicted load, stored energy and generating capability, do you need to provide additional generating capability to meet demand
Depending upon funding it might be developed for a number of different scenarios. There would be commonalities between the software, but hardware might be different.
  • Space station or colony energy management
  • Prosumer (consumers of electricity that produce) control of equipment on earth
  • Micro grid scenarios
